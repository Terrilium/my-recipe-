Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 cup sugar
  3. 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  4. 1 cup milk
  5. 2 large eggs
  6. 1 tsp vanilla extract

Helpful Cooking Advice

For a moist and fluffy cake, make sure not to overmix the batter. Always check for doneness with a toothpick, and let the cake cool completely before slicing for the best texture.
